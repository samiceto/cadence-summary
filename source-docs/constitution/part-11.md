# Constitution — Part 11: Growth Strategy (Cadence)

**Wedge:** "Get paid faster without chasing — and without losing clients." Land via accounting marketplaces where intent is highest.

**Acquisition:**
- **Marketplaces:** QuickBooks, Xero, Stripe app stores (primary, high-intent, low CAC).
- **Partners:** accountants/bookkeepers/fractional CFOs → reseller/referral tier (they manage many SMBs).
- **Content/SEO:** "reduce DSO", "automate invoice collections", AR benchmarks, ROI calculator.
- **Outbound:** targeted to AR-heavy verticals (agencies, wholesale, MSPs).

**Activation:** connect accounting in <10 min → instant DSO/aging insight + "at-risk invoices" → first sequence live same day (time-to-value = wow).

**Conversion:** free trial / freemium (read-only insights free; activation = paid); ROI anchored to recovered cash.

**Retention/expansion:** NRR via invoice-volume growth, email→SMS upgrade, payment plans, multi-entity, success fees; QBR-style monthly "cash recovered" reports.

**Referral/virality:** every payment request can carry light branding; accountant network compounding.

**Moats:** proprietary payment-behavior data → better predictions; deep accounting integrations; relationship-safety reputation; switching cost via embedded workflows.

**Metrics:** CAC payback < 5 mo; NRR > 115%; marketplace install→activation rate; partner-sourced %.

**Consistency check:** matches ICP (Part 2), pricing (Part 3), trust posture (Part 7), north-star (Part 12).
