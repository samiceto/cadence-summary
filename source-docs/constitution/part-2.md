# Constitution — Part 2: Customer Profile (Cadence)

**ICP:** B2B SMBs, $1M–$50M revenue, invoice-based or recurring billing, with aging AR and no dedicated collections team. Verticals: agencies, wholesale/distribution, MSPs, professional services, B2B SaaS, light manufacturing. Geo: US/UK/EU/CA first (English), expand with localization.

**Buyer:** Founder / Controller / Head of Finance. **Daily user:** bookkeeper, finance ops, founder. **Influencer:** external accountant/bookkeeper (also a partner channel).

**Jobs To Be Done:**
1. "Get my overdue invoices paid without me chasing them."
2. "Tell me which invoices are about to go late."
3. "Keep the tone professional so I don't lose the client."
4. "Reconcile payments automatically."

**Pains:** manual chasing, awkward conversations, cash-flow stress, no visibility, write-offs.

**Gains:** recovered cash, lower DSO, time saved, preserved relationships, forecastable cash.

**Anti-personas:** consumer/B2C debt; cash-only businesses; enterprises with existing AR suite.

**Willingness to pay:** high — ROI is literally recovered cash; budget in finance.

**Consistency check:** drives pricing (Part 3), distribution (Part 11), tone guardrails (Part 6/10).
