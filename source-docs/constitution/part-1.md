# Constitution — Part 1: Vision & Mission (Cadence)

**Version:** 1.0.0 · **Ratified:** 2026-06-17

**Vision:** Every B2B SMB gets paid on time without hiring a collections team.

**Mission:** Deploy an autonomous AR agent that predicts late invoices and runs personalized, relationship-safe dunning across channels — cutting DSO 20–40% within 90 days.

**Why now:** ~78% of finance leaders cite poor cash flow / high DSO as their top pain; AI dunning + cash application cut DSO 10–33 days; enterprise incumbents leave SMB under-served.

**Strategic principles (non-negotiable):**
1. **Cash is the product** — every feature ties to faster collection or protected relationships.
2. **Relationship-safe by default** — never harm a customer relationship to chase a dollar.
3. **Autonomy with guardrails** — the agent acts, but within human-set policy and approvals for sensitive steps.
4. **ROI in 30 days** — value must be provable within the first month.
5. **Margin-first, solo-operable.**

**What we are NOT:** not a debt-collection agency; not enterprise AR (HighRadius-class); not a generic invoicing tool.

**North-star:** **DSO reduction** (and recovered cash) per customer.

**Consistency check:** aligns with business model (Part 3), AI agent strategy (Part 6), metrics (Part 12).
