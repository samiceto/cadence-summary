# Constitution — Part 12: Success Metrics (Cadence)

**North-star:** **recovered cash per tenant per month** (and DSO reduction) — directly tied to customer ROI.

**Customer outcome KPIs:**
- DSO reduction (target 20–40% within 90 days).
- % overdue invoices recovered; aged-debt (>90d) recovery rate.
- Time saved on manual chasing (hrs/week).
- Relationship safety: complaint/unsubscribe rate < 0.1%; zero VIP/blocklist violations.

**Product KPIs:**
- Activation rate (connected + first sequence live) > 60%.
- Sequence engagement: reply rate, promise-to-pay rate, payment-link conversion.
- AI quality: tone-safety pass 100% gate; intent classification accuracy > 90%; collection lift vs control > 15%.
- Reconciliation accuracy > 99.9%.

**Business KPIs:**
- MRR / ARR; blended ACV ~$4,200; logo + net revenue retention (NRR > 115%).
- Gross margin > 85%; AI+comms COGS < 12% revenue.
- CAC payback < 5 mo; marketplace + partner-sourced pipeline %.

**Reliability KPIs:** 99.9% uptime; deliverability (inbox placement, bounce/complaint within limits); no message loss/duplication.

**Targets:** MRR $30–70k within first cohorts (per summary); expansion via volume + success fees.

**Review cadence:** weekly product/quality; monthly business + cohort retention + recovered-cash reporting.

**Consistency check:** measures vision (Part 1), business model (Part 3), product/AI quality (Parts 4/6/10).
