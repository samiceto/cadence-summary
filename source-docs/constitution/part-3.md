# Constitution — Part 3: Business Model (Cadence)

**Revenue model:** recurring SaaS tiered by AR volume / invoices managed, optional success fee on recovered aged debt.

| Tier | Price | For | Limits |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $99/mo | solo/micro | ≤100 active invoices, email only |
| Growth | $349/mo | growing SMB | ≤1,000 invoices, email+SMS, payment plans |
| Pro | $899/mo | mid-market | unlimited, multi-entity, API, priority |

**Add-ons:** success fee (e.g., 2–5% of recovered debt >90 days, opt-in); extra SMS bundles; reseller (accountant) tier.

**Pricing principles:** anchor to recovered cash ("pays for itself in 30 days"); annual default; land Starter/Growth, expand via volume + success fees + multi-entity.

**Unit economics (target):** blended ACV ~$4,200; gross margin **>85%**; CAC payback < 5 months via marketplace distribution; NRR >115%.

**Channels:** QuickBooks/Xero/Stripe app marketplaces (high-intent), accountant/bookkeeper partners, content on "reduce DSO".

**Cost discipline:** AI + comms COGS < 12% revenue; cheap models for routing/drafting, frontier only for negotiation/edge reasoning; SMS via Twilio metered + bundled.

**Consistency check:** supports vision affordability (Part 1), ICP WTP (Part 2), metrics (Part 12).
