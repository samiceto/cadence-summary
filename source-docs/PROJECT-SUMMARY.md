# Project 2 — Cadence

**Category:** Fintech-Ops Automation · AI Agent · B2B

## Project Summary

- **Name:** Cadence
- **Elevator Pitch:** Cadence is an **autonomous accounts-receivable agent** that gets B2B SMBs paid faster. It connects to your accounting system, predicts which invoices will go late, and runs personalized, polite-but-persistent dunning across email and SMS — escalating, negotiating payment plans, and reconciling payments automatically — cutting DSO by 20–40% without hiring a collections team.
- **Problem Solved:** ~78% of finance leaders say poor cash flow / high DSO is their biggest pain. SMBs chase invoices manually or not at all; enterprise AR tools (HighRadius-class) are too expensive and heavy for them.
- **Target Customer:** Founders, controllers, and finance ops at B2B SMBs ($1M–$50M revenue) with recurring or invoice-based billing — agencies, wholesalers, MSPs, professional services, B2B SaaS.
- **Target Industry:** B2B services, wholesale/distribution, agencies, SaaS, manufacturing.
- **Revenue Model:** SaaS subscription tiered by AR volume / invoices-managed — Starter $99/mo, Growth $349/mo, Pro $899/mo — optional success fee on recovered aged debt.
- **Competitive Advantage:** An agentic collections brain tuned on payment-behavior data → per-customer optimal contact timing/channel/tone, plus deep QuickBooks/Xero/Stripe marketplace distribution. ROI is self-evident (recovered cash), making it the easiest of the four to sell.
- **Market Score:** 9 / 10
- **Competition Score:** 7 / 10 (enterprise-heavy incumbents; SMB layer under-served)
- **Estimated MVP Time:** 7–9 weeks
- **Estimated MVP Cost:** $3,500–$6,000 out-of-pocket
- **Estimated Monthly Cost (at ~50 customers):** $600–$1,100
- **Estimated Monthly Revenue Potential (12-mo target):** $30k–$70k MRR

## Assumptions
- Accounting-marketplace listings (QuickBooks/Xero) provide low-CAC, high-intent distribution.
- "Pay for itself in recovered cash within 30 days" is the core, provable pitch.
- Deliverability + tone control (never harming customer relationships) is the trust bar.
